    About Project

    Beauty Angeli on of the leading beaty studios in Sliema had requested us to renovate the solon in just 5 days. This task was completly in slightly more than 3 days, and then it took a few more days to finish up with the painting. Our task was to remove walls, build new walls, build up stage for chairs from wooden frame, and lay laminate in the entire place.

    With great dedication to our customers satisfaction we have started the task on 1st of January when everything was closed in order to minimize closed days and losses for the beaty solon. In almost no time the studio was up and running. See the vidoe of how part of the task was handled.
