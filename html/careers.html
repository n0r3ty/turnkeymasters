    Job Description

    We are looking for a New Construction Sales Representative who will successfully manage the neighborhoods and individual home sites where CPI Security provides new construction installations. The goal is to generate new customer account revenue through the sale of security and low-voltage solutions to homeowners and builders using appropriate and approved means of account origination. This is a fantastic opportunity to earn a great performance-based income, while providing a valuable service to our customers.

    Key Responsibilities

  • Call on homeowners that have purchased homes from builders that participate in the CPI Builder program
  • Manage sales center relationships in communities using our services
  • Work with individuals building a new home to assist with their needs
  • Prospect in new construction areas
  • Accurately and efficiently complete all sales transactions within company standards and guidelines to meet or exceed sales expectations
  • Identify, develop and maintain positive builder relationships
  • Locate and attend builder sales events and production meetings to create additional account generation opportunities
  • Maintain excellence in all customer relations according to company best practices and core values
  • Meet all expectations regarding customer service best practices
  • Actively participate in searching goverment tendor contracts
