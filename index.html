    About Us

    We established our construction firm in Malta in 2019 late October and in just such short period of time we have managed to participate in different projects. Even though we have only recently joined Maltese market, we are also known as ECO-CLASS Ltd. in Moscow, where we used to operate for many years. We specialized in Emergency Consturions which means that we had to finish tasks quick when other contractors were late on their tasks, we made the job on time with high quality.

    What distingueshes us from all other companies is that we have a very long relationship with our workers. We have been together with them for more than 20 years and we can vouch for every single one of them. TurnkeyMasters team is experienced in many consturction areas and have built Hotels, Metro-stations, Bridges, Churches and Luxary-Villas. Our team of proffesionals specializes in many areas such as tilying, welding, brick laying, plastering and painting.
