    About Project

    During our work in Evolution Gaming in their Maltese branch. We were faced with many challenges. The initial task required us to dismalte their two floor of 600sq/m office space in just 30 days. The amount of work that there was included such tasks as removal of server rooms, partitions, gympsum walls, sound instalation and raised flooring. We had to solve many logistical problems such as removal of waste, dismantle order etc.

    With effort of our team we have finished our task in only 19 days, thus saving the company time and money on extra days before contact expiration. As we believe in Eco Friendly Environment, we have made as little waste as possible, we accomplished this not by braking gypsum boards, but by carefull and gental dismantle of the entire office floor and stacked them in ordered fashion and then hand by hand carried them downstairs and loaded into trucks. This approach was great as other offices could continue work on the floor without the need to breath dust.
