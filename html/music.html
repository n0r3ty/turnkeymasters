    About Project

    We were aproached by self-starter musician who wanted to create a music studion in his own garage in Qawra San Pawl Il-Bahar. We have made the entire design and planned the project according to proffesional music studion standards and quality. We started the task by dismantle of the shelves which where in the garage and carried away all of the waste and unsed material.

    After that we continued by building up the wooden frame for the stage and recording room and intsalled big soundproofed window along with doors. Then we spent a lot of time on soundproofing the entire place with not just 1, but 3 layers of soundproofed layer. This makes the room extremly quite and protects microphones from getting any kind of unwanted noise. In the end we took a gentle touch on the interior design.
